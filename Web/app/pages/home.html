Welcome to DisCoverBand

As Alice Cooper once said "Every good band in the world was a cover band first". Starting a coverband can be hard, so we've come up with something that can kickstart your cover carreer! To start a coverband, you need bandmembers, playing different instruments, coming together and making music together. But what if you could all play music at home, record that "one magnificent play", and then combine it with other people's best recordings, to a full, multi-insturment cover of the song!

Upload

You've been practising this song for a while and are ready to share it with the world. Record it, upload it and you're off...

Merge

Search for other covers of the same song, but on other instruments, so you can recreate the band's setup. Pick the best covers, make sure they're synced and you're done!

Enjoy

Rate the other instrument-covers, download and listen to your newly created cover, and share it with the world.
